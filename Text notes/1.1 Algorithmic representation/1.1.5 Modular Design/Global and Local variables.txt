~ Global variable
	- A longstanding variable
	- Accessible throughout the program's lifetime ( main, subroutine )
	- Storage of the variable exist until program is terminated

~ Local variable
	- A variable that is defined in a subroutine/function/procedure
	- Can only be accessed and modified in the within the subroutine
	- terminates when subroutine ends
	- Memory allocation of variable occurs within the scope of the subroutine and is deallocated once subroutine ends
